Mexicana Universal USA is a pageant in the United States that selects the national representative for the national Mexicana Universal pageant (before called Nuestra Belleza México).
The National Organization has not produced any national winner for Mexico.
The current winner of Mexicana Universal USA is Sayra García from Sacramento, California but with Mexican roots in the state of Michoacán.
