Mary Matha Arts and Science College is a Government of India aided college in the State of Kerala that is affiliated to Kannur University and managed by the Syro-Malabar Catholic Diocese of Mananthavady.

The college was re-accredited in 2024 by the NACC at A+ Grade. According to the directives of the NAAC, the college has established an IQAC for the sustenance and enhancement of quality in all accredited higher education institutions in India.


History
The college was established in 1995.
