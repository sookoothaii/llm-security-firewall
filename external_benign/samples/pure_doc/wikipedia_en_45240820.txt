Shelby Lindley (born September 12, 1986) is an American actress who has provided voices for the English dubs of Japanese anime and video games. Her major voice roles include Tsumugi Kotobuki in K-On!, Chizuru Aizawa in Squid Girl and Hitomi Shizuki in Puella Magi Madoka Magica.
