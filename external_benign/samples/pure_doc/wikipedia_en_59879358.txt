Dulce Nombre de Jesús is a district of the Vázquez de Coronado canton, in the San José province of Costa Rica.


Geography
Dulce Nombre de Jesús has an area of 67.86 km2 and an elevation of 1,345 metres.


Demographics

For the 2011 census, Dulce Nombre de Jesús had a population of 9,744 inhabitants.


Transportation


Road transportation
The district is covered by the following road routes:

 National Route 32
 National Route 307
 National Route 309
