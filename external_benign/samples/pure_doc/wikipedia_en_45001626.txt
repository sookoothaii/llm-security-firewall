Aisha Syed (Urdu: عاعشہ سید)  is a Pakistani politician who had been a member of the National Assembly of Pakistan from June 2013 to May 2018.


Political career
She was elected to the National Assembly of Pakistan as a candidate of Jamaat-e-Islami Pakistan on reserved seats for women from Khyber Paktunkhwa in the 2013 Pakistani general election.
