Nivas Adithan is an Indian actor who has appeared in Tamil language films and TV shows. He has notably played Naanga, Taramani, Kaaka Muttai, Chithiram Pesuthadi 2, Radiopetti, and Regina. Nivas is the son of veteran actor and producer Adithan.


Early life
Nivas Adithan was born in Karaikal, Puducherry, India, into a family with a background in the film industry; his father, Adithan, was an actor and producer. 


Career
He was recommended into acting roles by choreographer Kala Master after a performance on the dance show Maanada Mayilada in 2010. Nivas subsequently made his acting debut in Tamil cinema in Selva's Naanga, playing one of the lead roles.
Nivas also played "behind the scenes" in Ram's Thanga Meenkal. In 2015, he appeared in the critically acclaimed Kaaka Muttai  (The Crow's Egg). He also played a supporting role in Radiopetti, which won the KNN Audience award at the 2015 Busan International Film Festival.
