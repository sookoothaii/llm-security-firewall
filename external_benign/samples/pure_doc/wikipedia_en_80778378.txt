Tampa Premium Outlets is a regional outdoor shopping mall located off of Interstate 75 in Wesley Chapel, Florida. The mall is owned by Simon Property Group, and features shops such as including Calvin Klein, Adidas, and Nike.
As of August 2025, Tampa Premium Outlets offers 114 stores, with two stores to open later in 2025, Ulta Beauty, and Cheesecake Factory, bringing the total to 116.
The Tampa Premium Outlets holds the annual Summer Rum and Seafood Festival, which started in 2021.


History
The land for the mall was bought on October 3, 2014, by Tampa Premium Outlets LLC for $14.1 million.
Tampa Premium Outlets opened on October 29, 2015, with the anchor store for the shopping mall being Saks Off 5th. At the opening, there was 103 stores.
On March 19, 2020, Simon Property Group closed the Tampa Premium Outlets to prevent spread of COVID-19. While the mall was expecting to be reopened on March 29, 2020, it did not reopen until May 4, 2020.
In 2023, Tampa Premium Outlets added five new stores;  Lacoste, Cabi, Zwilling J. A. Henckels, Golf Apparel Shop, and Tacos El Patron.
In December 2024, plans were submitted to Pasco County for the construction of a Cheesecake Factory. The store is set to open in 2025.
