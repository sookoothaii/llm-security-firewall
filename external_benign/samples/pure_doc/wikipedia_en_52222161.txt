"Vívela" (English: "Live it") is a song recorded by the Spanish singer-songwriter Pablo Alborán. The song was included on his third studio album, Terral. The song was released as the lead single from his second live album Tour Terral (Tres Noches en Las Ventas) (2015). It was released as a digital download and CD single and peaked at number 1 on the Spanish Singles Chart in October 2015.
