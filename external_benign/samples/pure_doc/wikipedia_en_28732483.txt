Mills River Chapel, also known as Mills River United Methodist Church, is a historic Methodist chapel located near Mills River, Henderson County, North Carolina. It was built in 1860â€“1861, and is a one-story, rectangular, vernacular Greek Revival-style church.  It features a front portico with four, square, recessed panel wooden columns.  Adjacent to the church is the contributing church cemetery, with gravestones dating from as early as 1824.
It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1988.
