Bob McLeod (5 May 1913 â€“ 3 July 1958) was a Canadian cyclist. He competed in the 1000m time trial and the team pursuit events at the 1936 Summer Olympics. In 1934, McLeod won a gold medal in the 10 mile cycling event at the 1934 British Empire Games with a silver medal in the time trial event.
He was married to Mary, with whom he had 3 children. Wesley, Robert and Lynda.
In 2015, McLeod was posthumously inducted into the Canada's Sports Hall of Fame.
