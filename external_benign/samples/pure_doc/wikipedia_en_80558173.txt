Little Moon of Alban is a 1964 American television play based on a script by James Costigan. It starred Julie Harris, who had been in a 1958 version, and Dirk Bogarde. It was directed by George Schaefer, who had also directed the 1958 version.
It was Bogarde's American television debut (although he had been offered the same role in 1958).
