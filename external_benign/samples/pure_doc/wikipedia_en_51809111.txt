Mithrax is a genus of spider crabs in the family Mithracidae.


Species
Mithrax contains the following species:

†Mithrax arawakum  Klompmaker, Portell, Klier, Prueter & Tucker, 2015
Mithrax hispidus  (Herbst, 1790)
Mithrax pleuracanthus  Stimpson, 1871
Mithrax tortugae  Rathbun, 1920
†Mithrax unguis  Portell & Collins, 2004
