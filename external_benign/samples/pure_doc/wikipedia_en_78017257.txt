Aarón Martín Luis (born 24 November 2006) is a Spanish footballer who plays as a midfielder for CD Mirandés, on loan from Saudi club Al Qadsiah FC.


Career
Born in La Orotava, Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Canary Islands, Martín represented UD Orotava, Atlético Barranco Hondo, Florida CF and CD Tenerife as a youth. He made his senior debut with the reserves on 25 February 2024, coming on as a second-half substitute in a 6–1 Tercera Federación home routing of CD Unión Puerto.
Martín made his first team debut on 29 September 2024, replacing Sergio González late into a 2–1 Segunda División away loss to CD Castellón. He scored his first professional goal on 11 October, netting the opener in a 3–2 home loss to Real Zaragoza.
On 3 February 2025, Martín was transferred to Saudi club Al Qadsiah FC, being immediately loaned back to Tenerife for the remainder of the 2024–25 season. On 4 August, after suffering relegation, he moved to fellow second division side CD Mirandés on a one-year loan deal.
