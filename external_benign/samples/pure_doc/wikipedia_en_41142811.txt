Jirsar-e Nowdeh (Persian: جيرسرنوده, also Romanized as Jīrsar-e Nowdeh; also known as Jīreh Sar) is a village in Ziabar Rural District, in the Ziabar District of Sowme'eh Sara County, Gilan Province, Iran. At the 2016 census, its population was 137, in 49 families. Down from 159 in 2006.
