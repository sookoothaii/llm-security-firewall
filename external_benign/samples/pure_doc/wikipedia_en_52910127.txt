Ali Abdulla Eid (Arabic: علي عبد الله عيد, born 18 January 1991) is a Bahraini handball player for Al-Najma and the Bahraini national team.
He participated at the 2017 World Men's Handball Championship and at the delayed 2020 Summer Olympics.
