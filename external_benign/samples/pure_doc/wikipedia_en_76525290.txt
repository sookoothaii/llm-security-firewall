Jan Muhammad Buledi (Urdu: جان محمد بُلیدی) is a Pakistani politician who is senator-elect for the Senate of Pakistan from Balochistan province.


Political career
Buledi was elected from Balochistan province during the 2024 Pakistani Senate election as a National Party candidate on a general seat.
