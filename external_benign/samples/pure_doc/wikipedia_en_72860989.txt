Midigo is located in Yumbe District of West Nile Region Northern Uganda. It is one of the Sub-Counties of Aringa North County in Yumbe District


Geographical location
Midigo is located on 3°36′38″N 31°14′38″E and 18 Km by road North of Yumbe Town which is the largest and the District Headquarters. It is located near the Uganda Boarder with South Sudan.
