Borovskoye Shosse (Russian: Боровское шоссе) is a station on the Kalininsko-Solntsevskaya line of the Moscow Metro. It opened on August 30, 2018 as part of line's Ramenki - Rasskazovka extension.
It is in the Novo-Peredelkino District of Moscow at the intersection of Prirechnaya Ulitsa and Borovskoye Shosse, after which the station takes its name.
