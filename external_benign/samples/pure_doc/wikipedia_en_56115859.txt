Shichang station (simplified Chinese: 石厂站; traditional Chinese: 石廠站; pinyin: Shíchǎng Zhàn) is a station on and the western terminus of Line S1 of the Beijing Subway, it was opened on 30 December 2017.


Station layout
The station has 2 elevated side platforms.


Exits
There are 2 exits, lettered A and B. Both exits are accessible.
