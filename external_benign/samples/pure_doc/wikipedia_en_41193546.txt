Gorgin (Persian: گرگين) is a village in, and the capital of, Gorgin Rural District of Korani District, Bijar County, Kurdistan province, Iran.


Demographics


Ethnicity
The village is populated by Azerbaijanis.


Population
At the time of the 2006 National Census, the village's population was 272 in 72 households. The following census in 2011 counted 247 people in 74 households. The 2016 census measured the population of the village as 231 people in 81 households. It was the most populous village in its rural district.
