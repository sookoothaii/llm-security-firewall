Słomczyn [ˈswɔmt͡ʂɨn] is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Grójec, within Grójec County, Masovian Voivodeship, in east-central Poland.
The Autodrom Słomczyn is a permanent motorsport circuit located near the town.
