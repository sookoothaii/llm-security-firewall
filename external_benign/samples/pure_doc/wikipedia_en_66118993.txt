Oussama Boughanmi, also known as Oussema Boughanmi, (Arabic: أسامة بوغانمي;born 5 January 1990) is a Tunisian footballer who plays as a midfielder for Lakeshore SC in Ligue2 Quebec.


Club career
In April 2023, he signed a deal with CS Saint-Laurent of Ligue1 Quebec on a one-year contract.
On 2 July 2023, Boughanmi joined Saudi club Mudhar. On 19 January 2024, Boughanmi was released by the club.
In 2025, he joined Lakeshore SC in Ligue2 Quebec.
